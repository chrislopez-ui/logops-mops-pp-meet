Key Issues for Discussion
⚠️ Volume Spike — 19 Delays vs 12 Prior Week (+58%)
The week of May 11 represents the highest delay count in the rolling 4-week window. The OOC bucket drove the increase (+7), while PostProd also ticked up (+1). The VQ Delay Rate chart (from 2.96% low on May 4 back up to 3.80% on May 18) confirms the trend reversal is real. Transit damage and CA compliance are the two new clusters not prominently seen in prior weeks.
🌴 California Smog Compliance — 3 OOC Delays in One Week
Three vehicles (Pathfinder, Wrangler 4xe, BMW M3) pushed for CA-specific emissions reasons at end-of-line hubs. The BMW M3 was flagged with an aftermarket tune and downpipe — this should be identifiable much earlier at acquisition or Recon intake. Recommend aligning with the Logistics team on adding a CA-routing pre-check before vehicles transit west.
🔧 PostProd ETA Discipline — 3 of 4 PP Delays are ETA Misses
The Challenger, Escape, and F-250 all missed committed repair ETAs. The FIAT 500 is a repair quality miss — the PP regulator swap didn't address all broken actuator components, causing a second push at the delivery hub. Market Ops should review ETA-setting process with vendor partners and flag repeat-ETA misers for escalation.
🚚 Transit Damage — 4 OOC Cases Including Two F-150s
The AMG E-Class (6-leg transit, windshield + fuel leak), F-150 sliding off hauler (22-day sit + brake glaze), Versa (radar bracket impact), and Explorer (hood latch failure) all show transit-related damage. The 2023 F-150 sat on a hauler for 22 days before delivery — e-brake glazing and air in brake lines are predictable outcomes. Logistics should review extended-dwell haul policies.
Recommended Action Items
🌴
CA Pre-Transit Screening Checkpoint LOGISTICS + MARKET OPS
Establish a CA-bound vehicle emissions pre-check (OBD readiness, aftermarket mods, known compliance flags) before vehicles depart PHX-IC for CA hubs. Prevents last-mile compliance delays at SD/TCA/LA hubs.
📋
PP ETA Accountability Review MARKET OPS
Review the 3 ETA-miss PostProd cases (Challenger, Escape, F-250) with vendor partners. Identify if ETAs were set unrealistically or vendor execution is the gap. Escalate repeat vendors.
🚛
Long-Dwell Hauler Policy Review LOGISTICS
The 2023 F-150 sat on a hauler for 22 days — resulting in glazed rotors and air in brake lines (OOC). Propose a max-dwell threshold for vehicles in transit that triggers a re-inspection before delivery attempt.
🔎
Aftermarket Mod Flagging at Intake RECON / MARKET OPS
The BMW M3 with aftermarket tune and downpipe shouldn't have reached a CA hub without resolution. Add aftermarket powertrain/emissions mod detection at Recon QC intake for CA-routed vehicles.
🛠
FIAT 500 Repair Root-Cause Review MARKET OPS
PP repair on window regulator failed to address all broken actuator parts, leading to second delay at delivery hub. Review PP tech sign-off process for window/door actuator repairs — are all sub-components being inspected?
📊
Watch: VQ Rate Trending Back Up BOTH TEAMS
The overall VQ delay rate dropped to 2.96% (May 4) but has risen to 3.80% by May 18. With 19 delays in the week of May 11, this week's data will likely push the rate higher. Monitor closely and set a shared goal to get back below 3.0% by end of May.
