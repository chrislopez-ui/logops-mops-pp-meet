🌡️ HVAC / AC — New #1 This Week
4 of 19 delays (21%) were HVAC-related — AC blowing hot, squealing pulleys, heater failures. This is the highest single category this week and not a top QTD driver, making it an emerging trend worth watching.
🔍 47% Missed at Inspection
9 of 19 delays surfaced within 0–3 days of a passing inspection. The pattern is consistent with QTD — issues are present at time of inspection but aren't being caught.
🚛 37% Caught at MOPS
7 of 19 delays were first identified at market ops or delivery hubs after leaving Tolleson. Oil leaks, tire punctures, and window/door issues arriving at the customer's door is avoidable.
⚙️ Aftermarket Parts — 2 Vehicles
Two vehicles were delayed due to aftermarket parts (intake, tune/modifications) that prevented smog or repair approvals. These should be flagged and resolved at Recon intake — not discovered at the hub.
🎯 Priority Action: Tighten Recon QC checklist — OBD scan for all engine codes, engine bay fluid check while running, full tire inspection (nails, sidewalls, lug nuts, TPMS), and battery load test before every vehicle receives a passing grade. Keep focus to protect the 2.8% QTD rate and stay below the 3.2% target.
